# 🇺🇦 Supporting Ukraine

Small steps that can make a big difference.

---

## 💵 Donate to Verified Fundraisers

- [United24](https://u24.gov.ua) - the initiative of the President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy
- [Come Back Alive](https://savelife.in.ua/en/) - biggest non-governmental foundation supporting the Armed Forces
- [KOLO](https://www.koloua.com/en/donate) - foundation for rapid support to the Ukrainian military
- [Hospitallers](https://www.hospitallers.life/needs-hospitallers) - volunteer organization of paramedics delivering life-saving support
- [Serhiy Prytula Foundation](https://prytulafoundation.org/en/humanitarian-help) - foundation by the well-known volunteer since 2014

> [!NOTE]
> 💡 Even the smallest donations count. United24 supports monthly credit card payments.

## 📣 Raise Awareness

- Share posts about the war on social media
- Find and join local rallies and protests
- Email local politicians, demand to: Arm Ukraine + Boycott Russian oil and gas + Recognise Russia as a terrorist state
